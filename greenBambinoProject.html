GREEN BAMBINo

Is the brand created for a Nutritional Therapist specialising in pre-natal and post-natal health, children's health, & fertility.

The name was developed with the client, along with the colour palette. The client wanted a muted green colour theme with calming greys.

The logo was chosen out of many different versions of digitally created vegetable drawings. The client wanted a watercolour style and we decided the peas worked best because of the links between a little peas in a pod, and babies & pregnancy.

This business wanted a strong brand identity for their instagram page so I created some highlight covers too.
